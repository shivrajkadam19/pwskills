of  Techelons'24

Techelons is one of the most important and well known technical festivals organized by P. R. Pote Patil Educational Group, Amravati under the guidance of Shri Pravin Pote Patil and Shri Shreyas Dada Pote Patil. In the last 10 Years of Techelons Journey, various well-known and world famous international guests have arrived and inaugurated the events. Techelons is the technical festival which provides a platform to the participants from multidisciplinary fields to show their talents and win exiting and bumper prizes. We welcome you all in Techelons 2024 at P. R. Pote Patil Educational Group, Amravati.
